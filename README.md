# AI-Powered UK Market Intelligence Assistant

## Project Objective

This project aims to build an AI-enabled market intelligence assistant that analyses UK business data and allows users to ask natural-language questions about company structure, business activity, regional concentration, and market patterns.

The goal is to move beyond static dashboards and create a system that combines data analysis, decision-support logic, and AI-generated explanation in a single workflow.

## Problem Statement

Public business datasets contain valuable information about how the UK market is structured, but they are often difficult to interpret for non-technical users.

This project addresses that challenge by creating a system that can:

- analyse structured datasets
- identify key business patterns
- summarise insights in plain English
- support data-informed market intelligence decisions

## Example Questions the Assistant Should Answer

- Which UK regions have the highest business activity?
- What company structures dominate the market?
- Which locations show strong business concentration?
- What patterns appear in company status and business stability?
- What actions could a business or analyst take based on the data?

## System Workflow

The project will follow this workflow:

Raw Data  
↓

↓  
Analytical Engine  
↓  
Insight Generation  
↓

↓

↓  
Decision-Support Output
